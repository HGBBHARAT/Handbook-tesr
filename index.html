ERLC Private Server Rules

Rule 1 | FRP: All roleplays must withhold a realistic standard and you must not do anything that could be deemed as unrealistic.
Rule 2 | VDM: Vehicle deathmatch is not allowed. This means you are not to shoot other vehicles without a valid reason to do so.
Rule 3 | RDM: Random Deathmatch is not allowed. This means you are not to shoot or kill anyone without a valid reason to do so.
Rule 4 | Gun Motion: You must use Gun Motion such as "grabs gun" or "slings" for every weapon apart from the pistol or taser. Keep in mind gun motion is only needed when pulling a gun on a law enforcement. Gun motion is not needed if you are pulling a gun at a civilian. Law enforcement do not need to use gun motion at all.
Rule 5 | Realistic Avatars: Your avatar that you wear in game must be realistic and resemble something that you may see in real life. Any morphs of your heads, bodies etc are not allowed.
Rule 6 | New Life Rule: NLR means that once your character is arrested or dies then you have no previous member as that character and cannot go roleplaying again with that character.
Rule 7 | Team Creation: The creation of smaller teams like CIA, FBI etc. isn't allowed. They are already a thing, Go into the discord to apply.
Rule 8 | Realistic Driving & Roleplaying Crashes: You must drive realistically at all times and you must roleplay all of your crashes no matter what speed they are at.
Rule 9 | Fear RP: Fear Roleplay is always in effect. For example, if someone is pointing a weapon at you, you must comply with their demands.
Rule 10 | Detain Requests: You must always accept detain requests. If you feel the arrest was unjustified, call staff afterwards.
Rule 11 | Pointless Open Carrying: Pointlessly carrying a gun around for attention isn't allowed. This is due to it causing issues & being misused.
Rule 12 | Cop Baiting & Yielding to Emergency Vehicles: Cop baiting is strictly not allowed. This means pointlessly trying to get police attention. You must not start pointless pursuits just because a cop is behind you. You must also yield to the side if any Emergency Vehicles are behind you.
Rule 13 | Robberies: You cannot go on a spree robbing everything you can as fast as you can [5 Minute Wait Time]. You must space your robberies out for realism. Also, if you are going to rob the bank, you must ask for staff's permission beforehand.
Rule 14 | Interrupting Roleplays: You must not interfere with on-going role plays. Don't cause a mess of someone else's scene.
Rule 15 | Priorities: When a priority is active, you may not do any sort of criminal activity that will make you wanted.
Rule 16 | Initiating Pointless Pursuits: You must always have a reason to initiate a pursuit, it cannot be just because you want to run from the police.
Rule 17 | Group Limits: Groups of more than 4 aren't allowed if you're committing crimes. You must not have more than 3 people when robbing the jewelry store. You must also not drive around in groups to try and get around this rule.
Rule 18 | SWAT Gear: The battering ram, Osiris Sniper, G36C, SWAT vest, Bearcat, & SWAT uniforms aren't allowed if you aren't trained in SWAT.
Rule 19 | VC: If the server startup selects VC (Voice Chat in discord) you must have a ready to use mic and be in a Discord VC by the time you are loaded into the server.
Rule 20 | Safe Areas: You are not allowed to commit crimes inside of the Fire Station, either of the LEO spawn points and the DOT station. If you do then you will be moderated by staff.
Rule 21 | Use of Uniforms & Liveries: You must use the official liveries and uniforms for our departments while you are inside of our server.
Rule 22 | Yielding to Staff Cars: If there is a staff car behind you with its lights on then you must either stop and pull to the side or come to a stop completely if it is stopping you.
Rule 23 | Display Names: If you use the display name feature in game, you must create a name for your character and use that. You must not have anything inappropriate and nothing which claims you as a staff member.
Rule 24 | Respawning: If you die you must call for EMS and wait 2 minutes before respawning if none show up. If there are no EMS to begin with you must respawn. If law enforcement were involved, call for a mod to heal you so you can resume going into custody.
