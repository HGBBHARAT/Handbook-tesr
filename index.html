Official Staff Standards & Guidelines

ERLC Private Server Rules

Rule 1 | FRP: All roleplays must withhold a realistic standard and you must not do anything that could be deemed as unrealistic.
Rule 2 | VDM: Vehicle deathmatch is not allowed. This means you are not to shoot other vehicles without a valid reason to do so.
Rule 3 | RDM: Random Deathmatch is not allowed. This means you are not to shoot or kill anyone without a valid reason to do so.
Rule 4 | Gun Motion: You must use Gun Motion such as "grabs gun" or "slings" for every weapon apart from the pistol or taser...
Rule 5 | Realistic Avatars: Your avatar that you wear in game must be realistic and resemble something that you may see in real life.
Rule 6 | New Life Rule: NLR means that once your character is arrested or dies then you have no previous memory as that character.
Rule 7 | Team Creation: The creation of smaller teams like CIA, FBI etc. isn't allowed. Use Discord to apply.
Rule 8 | Realistic Driving & Crashes: You must drive realistically at all times and you must roleplay all of your crashes.
Rule 9 | Fear RP: Fear Roleplay is always in effect. If someone is pointing a weapon at you, you must comply.
Rule 10 | Detain Requests: You must always accept detain requests. If unjustified, call staff afterwards.
Rule 11 | Pointless Open Carrying: Pointlessly carrying a gun around for attention isn't allowed.
Rule 12 | Cop Baiting & Yielding: No pointless pursuits. You must yield to the side for Emergency Vehicles.
Rule 13 | Robberies: 5 Minute Wait Time between robberies. Bank robberies require staff permission.
Rule 14 | Interrupting Roleplays: You must not interfere with on-going role plays.
Rule 15 | Priorities: When a priority is active, you may not do criminal activity that makes you wanted.
Rule 16 | Initiating Pointless Pursuits: You must always have a reason to initiate a pursuit.
Rule 17 | Group Limits: Max 4 for crimes. Max 3 for jewelry store robberies.
Rule 18 | SWAT Gear: Special gear (Sniper, G36C, Bearcat, etc) isn't allowed if you aren't trained.
Rule 19 | VC: If VC is selected, you must be in a Discord VC by the time you load in.
Rule 20 | Safe Areas: No crimes inside Fire Station, LEO spawn points, or DOT station.
Rule 21 | Uniforms & Liveries: Official liveries and uniforms only for all departments.
Rule 22 | Yielding to Staff Cars: If staff have lights on behind you, pull over or stop completely.
Rule 23 | Display Names: Realistic character names only. No claiming staff roles.
Rule 24 | Respawning: Wait 2 mins for EMS. If LEO involved, call mod to heal/resume custody.
