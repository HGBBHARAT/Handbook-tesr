ERLC Private Server Rules

Rule 1 | FRP: All roleplays must withhold a realistic standard and you must not do anything that could be deemed as unrealistic.
Rule 2 | VDM: Vehicle deathmatch is not allowed. This means you are not to shoot other vehicles without a valid reason to do so.
Rule 3 | RDM: Random Deathmatch is not allowed. This means you are not to shoot or kill anyone without a valid reason to do so.
Rule 4 | Gun Motion: Must use gun motion like "grabs gun" for every weapon except pistol/taser when pulling on LEO.
Rule 5 | Realistic Avatars: Your avatar must be realistic. Any morphs of heads or bodies are not allowed.
Rule 6 | New Life Rule: Once arrested or dead, you have no previous memory and cannot RP with that character again.
Rule 7 | Team Creation: Creation of smaller teams like CIA/FBI isn't allowed. Apply via Discord.
Rule 8 | Realistic Driving: You must drive realistically and roleplay all crashes no matter the speed.
Rule 9 | Fear RP: Fear RP is always in effect. If someone points a weapon at you, you must comply.
Rule 10 | Detain Requests: You must always accept detain requests. If unjustified, call staff afterwards.
Rule 11 | Pointless Open Carrying: Pointlessly carrying a gun for attention is not allowed.
Rule 12 | Cop Baiting: Strictly forbidden. Do not start pointless pursuits just because a cop is behind you.
Rule 13 | Robberies: 5-minute wait time between robberies. Bank robberies require staff permission.
Rule 14 | Interrupting Roleplays: Do not interfere with ongoing roleplays.
Rule 15 | Priorities: No criminal activity allowed while a priority is active.
Rule 16 | Pointless Pursuits: You must have a valid reason to initiate a pursuit.
Rule 17 | Group Limits: Groups of more than 4 for crimes, or more than 3 for jewelry store are not allowed.
Rule 18 | SWAT Gear: Gear like battering rams or G36C not allowed if not trained in SWAT.
Rule 19 | VC: If VC startup is selected, you must have a working mic and be in Discord VC.
Rule 20 | Safe Areas: No crimes at Fire Station, LEO spawns, or DOT station.
Rule 21 | Uniforms & Liveries: Official department uniforms and liveries must be used.
Rule 22 | Yielding to Staff: If a staff car has lights on behind you, you must stop or pull over.
Rule 23 | Display Names: Realistic character names required. No inappropriate names or claiming staff rank.
Rule 24 | Respawning: Wait 2 mins for EMS before respawning. If LEO involved, call mod for heal to resume custody.
